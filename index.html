Why Chess?

Chess, the most mysterious and fascinating of all games

Imagine your life as a game of chess. Sitting in front of the chessboard, you have many choices. If you think you are the master of your destiny, your game is determined by the moves you choose to make. So take the first step and learn how to move the pieces so that you can take control of your life. By making this choice, you will gain wisdom, resilience and concentration. Get ready for great adventures in life and on the chessboard, because you are a true warrior.
